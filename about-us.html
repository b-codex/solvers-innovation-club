About Us

What we plan to do?

We plan to work on filling in that void by engaging our members in scientific innovations. We are working hard to ignite students’ initiative in the S.T.E.M fields by conducting training, experiments, and problem-solving projects. In consequence, it has gathered interested individuals in further pursuit of applied knowledge.

Alignment with our school's vision

● We focus on experiments and projects so as to gain applied knowledge in order to help our community;

● We encourage teamwork among our members on skills such as problem-solving and active participation;

● We aim at producing valuable members of society by facilitating activities that require leadership skills.

● We prepare students to be competent 21st-century engineers.

What do we offer our members?

● Certification (that could lead to Msc scholarships and job offers)

● Job and training opportunities from our partners

● Attend training, discussion panels, and events hosted by the club

● Will be assigned to projects if seen fit

● Can approach both our senior and junior members for guidance or if they have a project

idea that will solve a problem.

● Critical thinking and problem-solving.

● Creativity and innovation.

● Information, media, and technology skills.

● Be exposed to new technological advancements at our disposal.

● Media coverage for projects that have a major impact on our community.
